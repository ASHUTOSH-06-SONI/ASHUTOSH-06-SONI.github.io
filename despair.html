Anyways, getting to the main point, I'm disappointed, a lot, and it's annoying the f*** outta me. Not gravitating away from the topic, I'm just journaling about my despair, this kind of acts like the corner of my mind that somehow is connected to the keyboard or the diary.

I tried to win in a lot of things, failed pretty much everything. Never been good at sports, never good at games, never been a prodigy at things like chess, art — and definitely NOT an avid reader, I absolutely hate the fact that I read a few pages of books maybe 4-5 and then I fall asleep. Academic journals are different, they require deep involvement, or nothing. There's no grey area of just reading the paper here or reading it like a story coz that defeats the whole point.
